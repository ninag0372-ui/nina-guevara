5Ø3 † REBORN is an anonymous indie alternative rock band that primarily works online. This group of four anonymous individuals prefer to have their real faces hidden from the public eye, instead choosing to credit themselves under their own online aliases.

They originally started off by making their own covers of already existing songs, but just last year decided to focus on pushing out their own original music, developing a style that contains intense and powerful lyrics that are meant to channel the deep frustration that can come from being forced to abandon a passion due to circumstances beyond their control
